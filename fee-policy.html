Fee, Cancellation & Refund Policy

Our focus is complete customer satisfaction. In the event, if you are displeased with the services provided, we will refund back the money, provided the reasons are genuine and proved after investigation. Please read the fine prints of each deal before buying it, it provides all the details about the services or the product you purchase.
In case of dissatisfaction from our services, clients have the liberty to cancel their projects and request a refund from us. Our Policy for the cancellation and refund will be as follows:

Cancellation & Refund Policy
* Cancellation can only be made via online mode.
* Refund is only granted if the Cancellation is made half an hour before and not within the ride’s starting time.
* No Refund shall be granted if the customer cancels the ride within half an hour of ride’s starting time.
* No Refund shall be granted if the customer does not picks up his ride on time.

For example :-    If Mr.X books ride for 1hr , from 09 to 10 am NOW
If he cancels ride at 09:25 ----- Refund Granted ,
If he cancels ride at 09:31 -------- No Refund ,
If he does not picks up on time ------No Refund .
